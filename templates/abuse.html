Report Abuse

We strive to maintain a safe and respectful environment on mdbin. If you encounter any content that you believe violates our Acceptable Use Policy, please report it to us immediately. Below are the steps and guidelines for reporting abuse.

What Constitutes Abuse?

Abuse includes, but is not limited to, the following types of content:

  • Illegal activities or content.
  • Defamatory, abusive, harassing, or threatening content.
  • Viruses, malware, or other harmful code.
  • Pornographic, obscene, or adult content.
  • Content that infringes on intellectual property rights.
  • Violence, hate speech, or discriminatory content based on race, ethnicity, gender, religion, or sexual orientation.

Steps to Report Abuse

To report abuse, please follow these steps:

  • Identify the content you believe is abusive.
  • Take note of the URL or any other identifying information of the content.
  • Prepare an email to our support team at mdbin@zxcv.fyi.
  • Include the following details in your email:
    • A description of the abusive content.
    • The URL or location of the content.
    • Any additional information that may help us investigate the issue.
  • Send the email to our support team.

What to Include in Your Report

When reporting abuse, please include the following details in your email to ensure a thorough investigation:

  • Description of the Content: A detailed description of why you believe the content is abusive.
  • URL or Location: The URL or specific location where the content can be found on mdbin.
  • Time and Date: The time and date when you found the content.
  • Additional Information: Any other relevant information that may assist us in investigating the issue.

We take all reports seriously and will investigate each one promptly. Thank you for helping us keep mdbin a safe and respectful community.
